The Founder

Built by Someone

Kitchen

"The future of food is internet-first, data-driven, and ruthlessly efficient."

Sukhmanpreet Singh, with over five years of experience in the F&B consulting and franchising sector, launched CloudurKitchen under Abiding Ventures in Punjab with a singular vision — to build internet-first food brands that operate efficiently under one roof.

Recognizing that 60% of restaurants fail within three years, he built a systematic, data-driven approach to food entrepreneurship. Our team of chefs and brand managers craft menus based on demographic insights, keyword demand, pricing strategies, and evolving food trends.
